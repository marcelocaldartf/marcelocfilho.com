## Sound Design for Documentary

Documentaries need a subtle touch. 'Wavelength' was about the deep connections between people across the ocean. My task was to provide a score that was felt more than heard.

## Intimate Foley

For the close-up shots of hands and letters, we recorded extremely delicate foley using custom-built condenser mics. This was to create a sense of being in the same room as the subject.

## Final Audio Mixing

The final mix followed EBU R128 standards for broadcast but maintained its cinematic dynamic range for theatrical screenings. We focused on spatial audio to keep the voices centralized.
