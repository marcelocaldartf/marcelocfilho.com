## Audio Branding Strategy

For this tech brand, the goal was to create a "human-centric" sonic identity. We moved away from synthetic bleeps and bloops toward warm, analog textures.

## The Sonic Logo

We developed a 3-note mnemonic that's now used in every digital interaction, from app startup to notification chimes. It's designed to be comforting yet distinct.

## Cohesive Identity

We also provided a complete sound library for their physical product line, ensuring that the physical buttons on their hardware matched the audio feedback in their mobile application.
