## Sound Installation Overview

'Cyberia' was commissioned for a solo exhibition at the National Art Gallery. It explores the concept of a "digital wilderness"—a sonic landscape that's constantly changing.

## Interaction Design

Using ultrasonic sensors, footsteps were translated into glitchy ambient swells. As more people entered the space, the "sonic biodiversity" of the installation would increase.

## Generative Audio Engine

The entire score was generated in real-time, meaning no two visitors would ever hear the same composition twice. Our system used Markov chain probability to maintain a specific "mood" while allowing for infinite variation.
