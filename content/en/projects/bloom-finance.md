## Project Overview

Project 'Ether' was a collaborative effort to redefine the sonic landscape of an psychological horror masterpiece. The goal was to create a soundscape that felt personal and responsive to the player's presence.

## Custom Foley Systems

We recorded hours of organic textures—from deep-sea hydrophone recordings to microscopic movements of rusted metal. These were then implemented using a custom procedural engine.

## Dynamic Scoring

The score wasn't just a background track; it was an actor. Using heart rate data (optional), the music would shift its frequency spectrum to maintain a constant state of tension.
